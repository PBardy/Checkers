Instructions: click on a checker to select it. Clicking on the selected checker again will unselect it.

Rules: Each player takes it in turns to move their pieces, with red starting first. Moves can only be diagonal and, whilst a piece is not kinged, can only move forwards. Pieces can only move forwards onto an empty tile. If their is an opponent's piece, which is diagonally adjacent, and the next tile along this diagonal is vacant. A piece can jump over the opponent piece and remove it from the board.

Win condition: remove all of your opponent's pieces. Or ensure they can no longer move on their turn.
